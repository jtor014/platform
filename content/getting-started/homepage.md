## Build real software. Direct AI to do it.

A free learning platform that teaches you to build websites, apps, and APIs by directing AI agents — and to read, inspect, and challenge everything they produce.

---

## What You'll Build

Real projects. Working products. Deployed to the internet.

**Project 1:** A professional business website — live at a real URL.
**Project 2:** A full-stack app with a database, API, Docker, CI, and dual AI review.
**Project 3:** An interactive app with forms and email — planned by you from a client brief.
**Project 4:** An app integrated with payment or map services — verified against real API docs.
**Project 5:** A multi-user system with roles and permissions — tested by trying to break it.
**Project 6:** A production-grade system with monitoring, backups, and handover documentation.

Six projects. Each one builds on the last. By the end, you'll have a portfolio of deployed projects and the delivery skills to contribute to real teams.

---

## How It Works

**You direct. AI builds. You verify.**

Every project follows the same loop. You'll do it dozens of times until it's muscle memory:

1. **Plan** — Give AI a brief and a template. Review what it produces. Catch scope creep and vague requirements before any code is written.
2. **Build** — Direct AI through tickets, one at a time. Each ticket: branch, implement, test, review, merge.
3. **Verify** — Open the product and test it like a real user. Try to break it. Check specific values against the spec.
4. **Deploy** — Ship to a real URL. Verify it works in production, not just on your laptop.
5. **Reflect** — What went well? What did AI get wrong? What acceptance criteria were missing?

Each week looks like: plan on Monday, build Tuesday through Thursday, deploy Friday, reflect. The loop gets faster each time because you've done it before.

## What Makes This Different

**You direct AI to write the code — and learn to read, inspect, and challenge it.** The skill you're building — managing AI output quality — transfers to any tech stack, any domain, any tool.

**Real projects, not exercises.** You deploy to real URLs, integrate real payment systems, and handle real edge cases. Nothing runs in a sandbox.

**Five business stories.** After the foundation projects, you choose a client arc — a service business, online store, content platform, event platform, or community directory. Each introduces unique directing challenges you won't find in tutorials.

**Free and open.** No subscription. No paywall.
